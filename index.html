Ares - The God of War

About Ares

Ares is the Greek god of war and courage. He is one of the twelve Olypians, and the son of Zeus and Hera.

Ares at war

Ares' Characteristics

  1. He embodies the physical valor necessary for success in war.
  2. He can personify sheer brutality and bloodlust, in contrast to his sister, the armored Athena.
  3. An association with Ares endows places, objects, and other deities with a savage, dangerous, or militarized quality.
